Query: What was the last bill to come into law?

**Query for Congressional Analysis**

- **Main Intent:** Identify the most recent bill that has been enacted into law.
  
- **Specific Details Required:**
  - Determine and provide the title and number of the last bill signed into law.
  - Include the date when the bill was signed by the President.
  - Provide a brief summary of the bill's main provisions and objectives.
  - Note any significant amendments or debates associated with the bill prior to it becoming law.
  - Identify the primary sponsors and key supporters or opponents within Congress.
  - Provide context if possible, regarding the impact or significance of the bill.
